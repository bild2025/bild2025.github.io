Islem Rekik

Director of the Brain And SIgnal Research and Analysis (BASIRA) laboratory and an Associate Professor at Imperial College London (Innovation Hub I-X), London, UK

Imperial College London (Innovation Hub I-X)

Biography

Islem Rekik is the Director of the Brain And SIgnal Research and Analysis (BASIRA) laboratory and an Associate Professor at Imperial College London (Innovation Hub I-X). She is the awardee of two prestigious international research fellowships (EU Marie Curie Fellowship & TUBITAK 2232).
